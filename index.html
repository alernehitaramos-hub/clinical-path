About Clinical Path

Clinical Path is conceived as an educational simulator that allows users to explore structured clinical reasoning pathways through simulated consultations and academic scenarios.

Purpose

To provide a professional and accessible environment for the study and practice of clinical reasoning in educational contexts.

Audience

Suitable for students, instructors, academic institutions, and professionals who wish to reinforce analytical skills in training settings.

Key Features

Designed to communicate the value of the application with a polished and trustworthy presentation.
